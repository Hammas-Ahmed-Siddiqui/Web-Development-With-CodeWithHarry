vmin and vmax are two viewport units that are particularly useful for creating responsive layouts.
vmin stands for viewport minimum. It is equal to 1% of the smaller dimension of the viewport. So, if the viewport is 500px wide and 1000px high, 1vmin would be equal to 5px.
vmax stands for viewport maximum. It is equal to 1% of the larger dimension of the viewport. So, in the same example, 1vmax would be equal to 10px.
